Introduction

The Kiromoto Shivinki equation is defined as

$$ \begin{align} \frac{\partial u}{\partial t} &= \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^4 u}{\partial x^4} \end{align}. $$

It models the dynamics of a flame over time.
